Lasagna recipe

Description

There is are recipe for the best lasagna in the world

Lasagna Indgredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 1 (15 oz) container ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley (optional, for garnish)

Cooking steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, brown the ground beef or sausage over medium heat. Drain excess fat, then stir in marinara sauce. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta, 1 cup mozzarella, Parmesan, egg, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  4. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Layer 3 noodles, half the ricotta mixture, and more sauce. Repeat layers, ending with noodles and remaining sauce on top.
  5. Sprinkle remaining mozzarella on top. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes, until bubbly and golden.
  6. Let cool for 10 minutes, garnish with fresh herbs if desired, and serve. Enjoy!
